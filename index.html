About

I am currently an M.Sc. student in Electronic Information at the University of Science and Technology of China. My research interests lie in artificial intelligence, medical image analysis, multimodal learning, and deep learning for healthcare applications.

My recent work focuses on data-efficient learning for medical imaging, including multimodal prediction with PET/CT and self-supervised learning for 3D ultrasound analysis.

Research Experience

  • Shenzhen Mindray Research Project
    Worked on data-efficient self-supervised learning for 3D ultrasound analysis. The related manuscript, When Grouped Cyclic Shift Meets Masked Image Modeling: Effective Pre-training for Data-scarce 3D Ultrasound Analysis Tasks, is currently under major revision at Medical Image Analysis.
  • Deep Learning Model for Preoperative Prediction of Lymph Node Metastasis in Prostate Cancer
    Served as algorithm lead in a medical-engineering collaborative project. Built a multimodal deep learning pipeline for PSMA PET/CT analysis using 3D ResNet, transfer learning, and SVM classification. The model achieved an AUC of 0.89 and improved predictive accuracy over conventional baseline models.
